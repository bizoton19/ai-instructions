Consumer Product Recalls

Consumer product recalls are managed by the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC). The CPSC protects the public from unreasonable risks of injury or death associated with consumer products. Visit the CPSC website to search the full recall database, subscribe to email alerts, and report unsafe products.

Find the latest recall information on CPSC.gov. The recall database is updated weekly. Subscribe to receive email alerts for new recalls as they are announced.

U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission

The CPSC recalls household items, children's products, furniture, electronics, toys, appliances, clothing, and thousands of other consumer goods. Search the full recall database or subscribe to recall email alerts.

Visit CPSC Recalls

Report an Unsafe Product

If you have been injured by or know of a dangerous consumer product, report it to the CPSC through SaferProducts.gov. Your report may trigger a recall investigation.
