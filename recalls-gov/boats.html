Boat Recalls

Search the U.S. Coast Guard's database for recalls of recreational boats, personal watercraft, inflatable flotation devices, and other marine equipment.

Boat recalls are issued under the Federal Boat Safety Act

If your boat or marine equipment is recalled, the manufacturer is required to notify registered owners and provide a remedy at no cost.
