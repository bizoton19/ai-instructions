Recalls.gov

To provide better service in alerting the American people to unsafe, hazardous or defective products, six federal agencies with vastly different jurisdictions have joined together to create www.recalls.gov — a "one stop shop" for U.S. Government recalls.

Report a Dangerous Product

Each federal agency has its own system for reporting unsafe or defective products.

  • Consumer products: Report on SaferProducts.gov
  • Vehicles and car seats: NHTSA complaint portal
  • Food and drugs: FDA MedWatch
  • Meat and poultry: USDA FSIS Hotline
