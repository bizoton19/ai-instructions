Motor Vehicle Recalls

Search NHTSA's official database by VIN, license plate, or vehicle year, make, and model. Includes recalls for cars, trucks, motorcycles, tires, and child safety seats.

Check your vehicle using your VIN

Your 17-character Vehicle Identification Number is located on the lower left of your windshield, the driver's side door jamb, and your insurance card.

Search Vehicle Recalls by VIN

Enter your vehicle's VIN or license plate to check for open recalls. Get manufacturer-specific recall information and instructions for getting your vehicle repaired.
