Medicine Recalls and Drug Safety Alerts

Recalls of prescription drugs, over-the-counter medications, medical devices, and biological products are regulated by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.

Do not stop taking a prescription medication without consulting your doctor

If your medication is recalled, contact your pharmacist or physician for guidance. Alternative medications are usually available.
