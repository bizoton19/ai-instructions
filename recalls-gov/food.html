Food Recalls and Safety Alerts

Food safety recalls are managed by the FDA for most packaged foods and the USDA FSIS for meat, poultry, and egg products. FoodSafety.gov consolidates information from both.

Do not eat or use recalled food products

If you have a recalled product at home, discard it or return it to the place of purchase. Do not taste it to determine if it is safe.

All Food Recalls and Outbreaks

FoodSafety.gov is the federal government's combined resource for food recalls and outbreaks. Content is provided by the FDA, USDA/FSIS, and CDC. Subscribe for email or text alerts.
