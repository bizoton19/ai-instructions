Environmental Product Recalls

Recalls and safety actions for pesticides, herbicides, rodenticides, and other environmentally regulated consumer products are managed by the U.S. EPA.

Dispose of recalled pesticides and chemicals safely

Do not pour recalled chemicals down drains or into trash. Contact your local household hazardous waste program for disposal guidance.

EPA

EPA Consumer Product Enforcement

The EPA's enforcement division handles recalls, stop-sale orders, and cancellations for pesticides, rodenticides, and other regulated chemical products sold to consumers.

Pesticide Registration and Safety

All pesticides sold in the United States must be registered with the EPA. Find information on registered pesticides, label requirements, and safety restrictions.

Consumer Products with Environmental Hazards

Some products pose both safety and environmental hazards. The CPSC coordinates with the EPA on recalls of generators, gas-powered equipment, and chemical-containing items.
